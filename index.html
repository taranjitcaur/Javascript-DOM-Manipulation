GAME RULES:


- In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he wishes. Each result gets added to his previous scores and that results to his `cummulative score.


- But, if the player rolls a number 1, all his cummulative score will be lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.


- The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his cummulative score for his current turn gets added to his total game score . After that, it's the next player's turn.


- The first player to reach 100 points wins the game.
